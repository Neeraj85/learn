The World War Two

Description

The world war two was essentially fought between Hitler and Stalin, although many countries did participate in it. It was basically a war between Germany and Russia. There were countries that were helping out both of the sides. Germany was helped by Italy and Japan, where as Russia was being helped by England, France and USA. The alliance of Germany, Italy and Japan was known as the Axis, whereas the alliance of Russia, USA and England was known as the allies.

Main fighters

Allies
  1. Russia
  2. USA
  3. England
Axis
  1. Germany
  2. Japan
  3. Italy

Result

The result of the war was that the Allies ended up emerging as victorious. Hitler had to commit suicide in the end.
